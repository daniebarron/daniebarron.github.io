About Me

Hi, I'm Daniel Barron, a Theoretical Physics student at Trinity College, Dublin. I have a great interest in research, having done two research projects already, and I'm looking forward to doing more research in the future. My key skills are in problem solving, especially physics related problems, and Python, having done research projects related to Density Functional Theory and Natural Language Processing. In my free time I play Gaelic Football and guitar, and am the current PLANCKS Captain for the Theoretical Physics Students Association of Ireland.
